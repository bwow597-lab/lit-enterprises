Sparks Ignited

Impact, in real lives.

Behind every venture is a person who felt something change — a player who found their grit, an audience that became a community, a maker whose craft found its stage. These are the human stories at the centre of what we build.

Featured — Sports

From the longest-running club to the fastest-growing sport.

LIT TLP carries decades of community heritage — Hong Kong's longest-running club — into the city's pickleball boom. What began as a gathering place is now a launchpad for professional athletes and a home for players of every level.

It is proof of the LIT belief that joy compounds across generations: the same spark that lit up a court decades ago is now igniting an entire sport.
