Where
Global market with specific ties to French vineyards (Champagne and Bordeaux) and Hong Kong collaborations.
Who it's for
Wine aficionados, collectors, and environmentally conscious consumers who value substance, rare vintages, and premium eco-friendly products.

What it is

Verde Vines is a curator and provider of premium, eco-friendly, and rare wines sourced from family estates and exclusive vineyards. They bridge the gap between traditional winemaking excellence and modern sustainability, offering a carefully selected portfolio that includes champagne, sake, and limited-edition collaborations.

What they offer

  • Rare and Hard-to-Source Wines
  • Eco-Friendly Premium Wines
  • Champagne (Cédric Guyot Extra Brut Reserve, Rosé Brut Tradition)
  • Cadillac 2018 (Chateau Puy-Bardens)
  • Sake (19Dai Picasso Limited Supply)
  • Cognac (Deau Louis Memory Hors d'Age)

Why it stands out

  • Offers rare, hard-to-source wines alongside premium eco-friendly options.
  • Collaborates with notable figures, such as the 'MW Flying Vines' collection with Hong Kong actor Michael Wong.
  • Focuses on family-run vineyards and traditional winemaking processes, such as the Champagne Cédric Guyot estate.
  • Provides a curated 'Plan B' collection for those who trust their own instincts and palate over showmanship.
