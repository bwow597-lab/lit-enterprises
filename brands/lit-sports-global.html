Where
Headquartered in Hong Kong, serving the Greater Bay Area and the broader Asian market.
Who it's for
Professional athletes, sports investors, brand partners, and the sporting communities across Asia, including youth and underprivileged groups served by their foundation.

What it is

LIT Sports Global is a Hong Kong-based sports management and investment company driving the growth of emerging and established sports through elite talent development, strategic partnerships, and world-class events. As the sports arm of TGG Holdings, they specialize in identifying high-growth sports ecosystems and building scalable platforms across Asia and beyond.

What they offer

  • Professional Athlete Representation and Management
  • Sports Investment and Venture Building (e.g., LIT TLP Pickleball Club)
  • Tournament and Event Production (e.g., WPC, LUZZ Championship)
  • Media and Entertainment Content Production (Documentaries and Feature Films)
  • Strategic Brand Activations and Merchandise Design
  • Community Impact Programs and Sports Clinics

Why it stands out

  • Operating under TGG Holdings, a single-family-office investment platform focused on alternative asset classes.
  • Significant market reach with over 70 million audience reach and 900+ activated athletes across their platforms.
  • Multi-disciplinary approach that bridges sports with media, culture, and luxury through film production and high-end merchandise.
  • Dedicated social impact through the LIT Sports Foundation, focusing on mental wellness (Picklemind) and community clinics for SEN children.

Part of the family
