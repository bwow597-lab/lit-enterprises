Where
Hong Kong (headquartered with a +852 contact number and historical roots dating back to its first store opening in 1967).
Who it's for
Luxury jewelry collectors, sports fans and collectors of Hong Kong Sevens merchandise, and individuals looking for high-quality, crafted jewelry for daily wear or special occasions.

What it is

Lit by Larry is a contemporary jewelry brand that emerged as a "new light" and testament to happiness, drawing inspiration from its sister company, Larry Jewelry Limited. The business focuses on carrying forward a 60-year legacy of exquisite craftsmanship through a curated selection of fine gemstone jewelry and exclusive commemorative merchandise.

What they offer

  • Diamond and gemstone rings (Asscher Cut, Emerald Cut, Colombian Emerald)
  • Classic and Timeless Halo diamond earrings
  • Limited edition Hong Kong Sevens 50th Anniversary merchandise (necklaces, pins, cufflinks, magnets)
  • Every Day Diamond Sets and fine jewelry collections (Bracelets, Necklaces, Rings)
  • Professional jewelry design and consultation services

Why it stands out

  • Official merchandise partner for the 50th Anniversary of the Hong Kong Sevens.
  • A subsidiary or "new chapter" of the prestigious Larry Jewelry Limited, leveraging a legacy of exquisite craftsmanship since 1967.
  • Specializes in high-grade materials including Colombian emeralds, Asscher cut diamonds, and VVS-VS clarity stones.
  • Provides personalized professional service with direct access to brand designers via WhatsApp.
