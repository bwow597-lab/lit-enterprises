Aap Ba Din

Chinese Private Kitchen (中式私房菜) / Gourmet Cuisine (珍饈美饌)

Visit Aap Ba Din ↗ Back to ecosystem
Part of
LIT Enterprises
Where
Central, Hong Kong (1/F, Lan Kwai Fong Hotel @ Kau U Fong, 3 Kau U Fong).
Who it's for
Discerning diners looking for traditional Cantonese cuisine with a modern twist, as well as those interested in art and culture. The venue also targets corporate clients and private event organizers for business gatherings and private parties.

What it is

Aap Ba Din is a high-end Chinese private kitchen in Hong Kong that blends traditional Cantonese culinary culture with modern artistic expression. The restaurant offers a bespoke dining experience centered on the freshest seasonal ingredients and 'Chef's Choice' menus, all set within a space decorated with exquisite antique art collections to satisfy both the palate and the eyes.

What they offer

  • Chinese Private Kitchen (Private Dining)
  • Omakase-style Lunch and Dinner Menus (Chef's Choice)
  • A la carte Take-out Menu (Take Away)
  • Private Party and Business Gathering Venue Services
  • Traditional Cantonese Cuisine with features from other cuisines

Why it stands out

  • Omakase-style menu (Chef's Choice) based on the daily market's freshest and seasonal ingredients.
  • Integration of art and fine dining, featuring a collection of antique art pieces displayed throughout the restaurant.
  • Adheres to the culinary philosophy of 'Beauty, Taste, Pursuit, and Truth' (美，味，求，真).
  • Located within the Lan Kwai Fong Hotel @ Kau U Fong, offering a sophisticated and culturally rich dining environment.
