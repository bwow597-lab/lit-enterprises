Where
Global market with operating offices in the Czech Republic, Dubai, Netherlands, Hong Kong, New Zealand, and the USA.
Who it's for
Merchants and businesses across various sectors including E-commerce, Gaming, MLM, Retail, Food & Beverage, and Digital Products.

What it is

READIES is a next-generation payment orchestration platform that consolidates fragmented payment rails—including card acquiring, alternative methods, and crypto—into a single intelligent layer. The platform simplifies global commerce by providing businesses with smart routing, unified integration, and optimized checkout experiences.

What they offer

  • Card Acquiring Orchestration (PSPs)
  • Alternative Payment Orchestration (APMs)
  • Fiat-to-Crypto & Crypto-to-Crypto On-Ramp
  • Open Banking for instant bank transfers
  • READIES Vouchers for prepaid/cash transactions
  • READIES Pay App for mobile payment management

Why it stands out

  • Single integration providing access to over 200 payment solutions and 300+ global payment methods.
  • AI-driven smart routing and cascading to ensure higher transaction success rates.
  • Features the READIES token, a fixed-value digital asset (worth exactly €0.10) designed to eliminate crypto volatility.
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees for customers, ensuring the price displayed is the price paid.
  • Extensive physical presence with over 35,000 voucher outlets and a global network of 3,600+ merchants.
