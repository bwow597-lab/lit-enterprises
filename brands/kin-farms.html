Where
Active markets include China, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Hong Kong, and UAE.
Who it's for
Hospitality (luxury hotels and resorts), premium retail (specialty grocers), and food service/manufacturing (bakeries, ready-meal producers, airlines).

What it is

KIN Farms is a collective of producers with aligned values and a mission to combat the environmental impact of food systems. They identify farms with superior practices to produce premium ingredients, helping these farms differentiate and create routes to market through specialized sourcing, marketing, and distribution.

What they offer

  • Shell Eggs (Organic, Free-range, Cage-free)
  • Liquid Eggs (Whole, white, and yolk)
  • Egg Powder (Spray-dried whole, white, and yolk)
  • Superior Vannamei Shrimp (Raw and Cooked)
  • Glacial Lake Himalayan Trout (Smoked and Fillets)

Why it stands out

  • Transparent sustainability via the 'KIN Farms Health Report' and ESG scorecard.
  • Ingredients are tasted and validated by KIN Farms' own chefs.
  • High-altitude, pristine production environments (Himalayan trout at 2,600m and highland eggs at 1,800m),.
  • Commitment to low-carbon logistics, utilizing sea-freight and localized haulage.
  • First and only salmon trout farming company in China to receive ASC and BAP 4 Star certification.
