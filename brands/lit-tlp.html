Where
Hong Kong, specifically Tai Lin Pai Road Playground (大連排道遊樂場).
Who it's for
Beginners, youth, and competitive athletes in Hong Kong interested in pickleball.

What it is

TLP Pickleball Association of Hong Kong, China is a sports organization dedicated to popularizing pickleball through structured training and community events. They focus on cultivating youth talent, enhancing physical fitness, and training athletes to compete in international tournaments.

What they offer

  • Beginner Training Classes (covering basic skills, rules, and etiquette)
  • Advanced Training Classes (focusing on tactics, 3rd shot drop, and strategy)
  • Community Outreach and Sport Promotion Programs
  • Youth Development and Physical Fitness Training
  • Professional Athlete Cultivation for International Competitions
  • Pickleball Trial and Experience Sessions

Why it stands out

  • Achieved 5 gold and 5 bronze medals at the Thailand Phuket Asian Pickleball Open 2023.
  • Swept the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place titles in the 2022 Hong Kong Pickleball Men's Doubles Championship.
  • Led by Director Natalis Chan (陳百祥), a prominent Hong Kong celebrity and sports enthusiast.
  • Official involvement in major events such as the World Pickleball Championship Hong Kong Station.
