Asia-Pacific Boating

In-depth, insightful, informed: Asia-Pacific Boating is the leading source for news and features on luxury yachting and the ocean environment in Asia-Pacific.

Part of

Where
Asia-Pacific market, with historical roots in Hong Kong.
Who it's for
Luxury yacht owners, enthusiasts, high-net-worth individuals, and marine industry professionals within the Asia-Pacific region.

What it is

Asia-Pacific Boating showcases the latest yachts from the world’s top builders, highlighting the engineering and design of luxury superyachts. The publication provides in-depth coverage of the marine lifestyle, takes readers to exotic destinations, and serves as a leading voice for ocean conservation.

What they offer

  • Online news and feature articles covering yachts, industry, and lifestyle.
  • Bimonthly print magazine subscription.
  • Yacht sales and charter listings.
  • Comprehensive Marine Service Directory.
  • Organization of industry events and the Asia Boating Awards.

Why it stands out

  • Founded in 1976, making it a long-standing authority in the regional yachting industry.
  • Recognized as the leading source for news on luxury yachting and ocean protection in the Asia-Pacific.
  • Pioneer in showcasing exotic Asian destinations such as Raja Ampat, the Andaman Islands, and the Mergui Archipelago.
  • Strong commitment to ocean conservation through dedicated 'Blue Planet' features and collaboration with scientists.
