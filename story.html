Our Story

Where TGG stewards, LIT ignites.

Every great venture begins with a spark — a moment of joy, curiosity or connection that makes someone feel more alive. LIT Enterprises exists to create those moments, again and again, across everything we touch.

The spark philosophy

We believe everyone glows from within.

Our vision is simple: everyone glows with inner sparks from enjoying themselves. That belief shapes every brand we build, every experience we craft, and every life we set out to touch. The call to action is just two words — Be LIT.

Fun is the point

Joy is never a by-product. We design experiences that delight first, because a life fully lit begins with feeling good.

Creativity is craft

Original ideas, considered details. We treat every venture as a canvas — playful yet premium, never ordinary.

People are the centre

Connection over transaction, always. Behind every brand is a real person we want to see light up.

Part of the TGG family

Two roles, one greater good.

LIT Enterprises is the experiential front of The Greatest Good — the fun factory that brings people together. TGG Group is the financier and family-office backbone: the steward that governs, invests and sustains.

Where TGG provides discipline, patience and long-term stewardship, LIT provides energy, warmth and the human spark. Together, the family ignites The Greatest Good — building ventures that are both joyful and built to last.

What we do

Four pillars, one fire.

Our ventures span sport, lifestyle, media and wellness — each a different way to spark joy and connection in real lives.

Sports — grit & joy

Sport as a platform for connection, energy and belonging. We use play — led by pickleball — to build grit, perseverance and an inclusive sporting culture.

Lifestyle — live it well

Jewellery, wine, dining, nightlife and wellness — the art of a life fully lit, crafted around taste, occasion and the people who matter.

Media & Culture — stories that move

Talent, content and live culture that light up audiences — discovering artists and shaping the future of entertainment.

Wellness — energy for life

Longevity, mindful living and experiences that help people glow — precision wellness inspired by the science of living well.
